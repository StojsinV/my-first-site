The American M26 Pershing is an excellent tank – it’s maneuverable, has a good cannon and decent armour. But there’s always room for improvement, right? America’s military engineers thought so too. They equipped the Pershing with a new powerful weapon, welded a few steel plates and installed extra defense for the gun mantlet from the German Panther – and thus was born the T26E4 Super Pershing, a long-awaited addition to War Thunder coming in update 1.57.
